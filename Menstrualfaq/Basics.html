Menstrual cycle is part of your health, and you might want to track it because of clinical conditions such as thyroid issues PCOS, or because you are looking to conceive or just to keep track your general health. You can use the menstrual cycle tracker to track your periods and understand the changes that your body is going through. Along with tracking, Lori modifies your health plan when you log in your cycle and periods, to help you with menstrual discomforts like cramps and aches.
