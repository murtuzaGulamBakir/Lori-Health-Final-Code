Our testing process is fairly simple. We offer a pre-consult for us to understand your lifestyle, current food and fitness regimes and family history. A sample kit is shipped to you, and once you collect the saliva sample, you can ship it back to our labs. And don’t worry, the shipping is paid for by us. Once we have the sample in the lab, we run a multitude of test, and with the test results in hand, we prepare an action plan for you. Your assigned nutritionist reaches out to you for a comprehensive consult to walk you through your results and action plans and will help you make changes to your lifestyle, food, and fitness regime. We also will be following up with you for a period of 12 weeks to ensure you aren’t facing any issues and are able to transition to a path of ‘healthy living.’

For the testing and recommendation process, we are partnered with NMC Genetics, a state-of-the-art genomics laboratory led by a unique team of highly skilled molecular biologists, bioinformaticians and data scientists. NMC Genetics’ state of the art molecular laboratory follows international quality guidelines and standards for handling samples and DNA test processes. NMC Genetics is a subsidiary of NMC Healthcare LLC. the largest private healthcare company in the UAE and ranks amongst the leading fertility service providers in the world. NMC was the first company from Abu Dhabi to list on the London Stock Exchange and is now part of the premium FTSE 100 Index, an elite club of top 100 blue-chip companies by market cap. We are happy to help with more information if you would require. Please reach out to us at lori@lori.health.

Anyone can order a test. DNA tests give you information on your ability to digest nutrients, what kind of exercise suits you, what is your immunity profile, and if you are at a risk for diseases. Even kids can take the DNA test. It is painless and non-invasive.

Saliva sample is one of the best ways for us to obtain sufficient quantities of your DNA for our analysis. It is painless, non-invasive, and very convenient as compared to a blood sample.

The sample goes through various processes as part of the testing and analysis at our labs.

You can order a DNA test kit by accessing the “Buy” button in taskbar. Select the test that you would wish to order, and we will ship the kit to you at your address

We use courier services to ship the DNA test kit to you, and these services are available across India.

When you receive a test kit from us, you will be asked to provide your consent to your DNA sample (and data) to be included for our research purposes. If you do not wish us to include your data, simply "opt-out" of the same by selecting that option in the consent form. Except for your accountability coach, no one has active access to your information

The DNA results are based on scientific processes and the outcome is dependent on the quality of the DNA sample received from you. Please make sure to follow the instructions in the test kit during the process of sample collection. If the quality of the sample is sub-standard, the results will not be accurate.
