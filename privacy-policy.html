Lori Privacy Policy

Last updated on June 11, 2021

Your privacy is important to us. It is Lori Health's (also referred as “we” or “the Company”) policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect from you through our website and app, Lori. We are committed to protecting such information through our compliance with our privacy policies.

We only ask for personal information when we truly need it to provide a service to you. We collect it by fair and lawful means, with your knowledge and consent. We also let you know why we’re collecting it and how it will be used.

We only retain collected information for as long as necessary to provide you with your requested service. What data we store, we’ll protect within commercially acceptable means to prevent loss and theft, as well as unauthorized access, disclosure, copying, use or modification.

Your continued use of our app will be regarded as acceptance of our practices around privacy and personal information. If you have any questions about how we handle user data and personal information, feel free to contact us at lori@lori.health.

1. INFORMATION COLLECTED BY LORI

  1. We collect and store personal information that you provide as part of creating an account, as well as the information that is created/generated as part of you availing various services provided on the app.

With your permission, we may also record the browsing information such as how long you have visited a page on our website, what pages have you visited, ads that you have clicked, features that you have used, search terms that you have used and results that you have selected. This data is used for internal analytics and NOT for advertisement purposes.

For our DNA testing service, we obtain your written consent separately when we collect the test sample. We or our third parties may use this information for research purposes only when you consent to it
