Privacy

Your personalised health plan is primarily a nutrition plan. Based on all the information that you have given during the sign-up (e.g., your basic health information, your lifestyle, allergies and clinical concerns, choices and preferences, diagnostic tests), Lori plans a nutrition plan for you to be healthy and stay healthy. The nutrition plan offers you suggestions on morning drink, breakfast, lunch, and dinner. Each of the plans is curated for you, to help you be your best healthy self!

Yes, everyone gets a personalised health plan as soon as they sign up. Once the 7-day free trial period ends, you should subscribe to continue to access your personalised health plan.

We offer a wide range of cuisines and varieties that you can choose from. The dishes are easy to prepare and are tailored to your health needs. Crash diets are a concept that we don’t believe in. Rest assured your food suggestions are based on your lifestyle, food preferences, clinical concerns, allergies, and any other inputs such as DNA, that you have given us. The nutrition regime provides you with suggestions that you can easily make at home and make you feel well-fed. We have designed each of the dishes to provide you with the right kind of nutrition, both macronutrients (carbohydrates, proteins, and fats) and micronutrients (vitamins and minerals).

Next to every recommendation, we offer a chance to change the meal. Every suggestion comes with almost 9 alternate options for you to select. Pick any one of them in the place of your current recommendation, and the Lori app takes care of the tracking accordingly. Do write to us if you have a specific dish that you would like us to include in your regime at lori@lori.health. We promise to work on it!

Weight is not an issue but is a symptom of an underlying one. Lori promotes a healthy lifestyle, part of which is to help you manage your weight. The nutrition regime helps you healthily manage your weight and will never put you on any crash diets. You don’t lose weight and become healthy, but you become healthy and lose weight in the process. The funda is that once you start a health regime, your weight will be certainly under control. Cause and effect, my friend!

Every meal has the nutrient information displayed on the ‘recipe card’, and you can access it by clicking on the dish. The recipe card contains information on macronutrients such as carbohydrates, proteins, and fats as well as the micronutrients such as vitamins and minerals. Please let us know if you require any additional information.

The trackers in the app help track of your meals and the diet regime. You can track the information about macro nutrients as well as micronutrients along with calories intake.
